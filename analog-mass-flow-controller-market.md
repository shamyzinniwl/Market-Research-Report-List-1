<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog mass flow controller market is primarily categorized into two types: thermal and differential pressure types. Thermal mass flow controllers utilize a heat transfer principle, measuring the cooling effect of a fluid passing over a heated element, ensuring precise flow regulation. On the other hand, differential pressure mass flow controllers rely on the pressure difference across a constriction to calculate flow rates. Both types are essential in various applications, such as gas and liquid control in industrial processes.</p></p>
