<p><p>The silver-based antimicrobial agent market consists of various carrier types utilized to enhance the efficacy and stability of silver compounds. Titanium dioxide carriers provide photocatalytic effects alongside silver's antibacterial action. Glass carriers are favored for their non-reactive nature and durability, allowing for sustained release of silver ions.
