<p><strong>The Plate Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Cooler</li><li>Brazed Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plate coolers market primarily includes two types: frame coolers and brazed coolers. Frame coolers consist of multiple thin plates assembled within a frame, allowing for efficient heat exchange. They are versatile and easy to maintain, making them suitable for various applications, particularly in dairy and food industries. Brazed coolers, on the other hand, feature plates that are permanently joined together using a brazing process, offering superior thermal conductivity and compact design, making them ideal for high-pressure and compact applications.</p></p>
