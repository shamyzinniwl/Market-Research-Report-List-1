<p><p>The Pelton turbine is a type of impulse turbine widely used in hydropower applications, particularly suited for high-head water sources. It operates on the principle of converting the kinetic energy of water jets into mechanical energy, utilizing spoon-shaped buckets mounted on a rotating wheel. <p><p>The Pelton turbine market is categorized based on the number of nozzles: single nozzle, twin-nozzle, and multi-nozzle designs. Single nozzle turbines are simple and cost-effective, ideal for low-capacity applications. Twin-nozzle turbines offer improved efficiency and performance, making them suitable for medium-capacity needs. Multi-nozzle turbines maximize energy extraction from high-velocity water sources, catering to large-scale hydroelectric projects. Each type is tailored to specific operational demands and environmental conditions, ensuring optimal energy conversion based on water flow characteristics.</p></p>
