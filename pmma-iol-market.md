<p><p>The PMMA Intraocular Lens (IOL) market encompasses several types tailored for specific visual needs. Multifocal Intraocular Lenses offer multiple focal points, enabling clear vision at various distances, reducing dependence on glasses. Adjustable Intraocular Lenses allow post-operative adjustments for personalized vision correction, enhancing adaptability. Aspheric Intraocular Lenses feature a specialized design that reduces spherical aberrations, improving contrast sensitivity and overall visual quality. These innovations cater to different patient requirements, driving growth in the PMMA IOL market.</p></p>
