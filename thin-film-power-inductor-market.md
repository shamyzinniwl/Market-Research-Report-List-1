<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology</li><li>Through Hole Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thin-film power inductor market is primarily categorized into two types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT inductors are designed for compact circuit designs, allowing efficient integration on surface-mounted circuit boards, enhancing performance in space-constrained applications. Conversely, THT inductors offer robust connections through drilled holes on printed circuit boards, providing stability and suitability for high-power applications. Both types are essential in modern electronics, catering to varying design requirements and performance needs across industries.</p></p>
