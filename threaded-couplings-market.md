<p><p>The threaded couplings market is primarily divided into two types: threaded half couplings and threaded full couplings. Threaded half couplings are used to connect pipes or fittings, providing a male or female interface to facilitate quick assembly. In contrast, threaded full couplings offer a complete threaded connection, allowing two pipes to join securely. Both types are essential in various applications, including plumbing, oil and gas, and industrial machinery, due to their reliability and ease of installation.</p></p>
