<p><p>The low voltage power distribution market consists mainly of two types: fixed-type and drawer-type systems. Fixed-type systems feature a permanent, static design suitable for applications where minimal maintenance is required. This design offers reliability and durability, making it ideal for established infrastructures. In contrast, drawer-type systems are modular and allow easy access to individual components for maintenance or upgrades, providing flexibility in evolving power needs. Each type serves distinct operational requirements, influencing selection based on application specifics.</p></p>
