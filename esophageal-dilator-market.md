<p><ul><li>Mechanical Esophageal Dilators</li><li>Balloon Esophageal Dilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The esophageal dilator market comprises two primary types: mechanical esophageal dilators and balloon esophageal dilators. Mechanical esophageal dilators are rigid instruments designed to widen the esophagus, often used in endoscopic procedures to alleviate strictures. Balloon esophageal dilators, on the other hand, utilize inflatable balloons to gradually expand the esophageal lumen, offering a more controlled dilation method. Both types are essential in treating esophageal conditions, improving patient outcomes by facilitating easier swallowing and reducing discomfort.</p></p>
