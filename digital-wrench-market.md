<p><p>The digital wrench market encompasses three primary types: pneumatic wrenches, hydraulic wrenches, and electric wrenches. Pneumatic wrenches utilize compressed air for powerful torque applications, making them suitable for industrial use. Hydraulic wrenches operate using pressurized fluid, providing precise torque control and durability in challenging environments. Electric wrenches, driven by battery or corded power, offer convenience and portability, appealing to both professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Each type serves specific needs, contributing to diverse market demand across various industries.</p></p>
