<p><p>Digital Video Servers (DVS) are essential in video surveillance and broadcasting, leveraging various compression technologies for efficient streaming and storage. M-JPEG technology encodes video as a sequence of JPEG images, offering simplicity but at high bandwidth usage. MPEG-4 technology improves compression efficiency, allowing for smaller file sizes and better quality, making it suitable for online streaming. H.264 technology further enhances video quality and compression, revolutionizing video playback, and is widely adopted in modern applications for its balance of efficiency and performance.</p></p>
