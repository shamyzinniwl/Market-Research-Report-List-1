<p><ul><li>Jackup</li><li>Semi-Submersibles</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floating rig market comprises three primary types: jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile, self-elevating platforms ideal for shallow waters, featuring legs that anchor to the seabed. Semi-submersibles float on the water's surface, anchored by chains, offering stability in deeper waters. Drillships are advanced vessels equipped with drilling equipment, designed for deepwater exploration, characterized by their mobility and versatility. Each type caters to varying depths and environmental conditions in offshore drilling operations.</p></p>
