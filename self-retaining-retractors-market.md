<p><p>The self-retaining retractors market comprises two main types: single-use and reusable retractors. Single-use retractors are designed for one-time application, promoting hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them popular in surgeries requiring strict infection control. Reusable retractors, on the other hand, are built for multiple uses, offering cost-effectiveness over time and sustainability benefits. Both types serve crucial roles in surgical procedures, enhancing visibility and accessibility for surgeons while ensuring patient safety during operations.</p></p>
