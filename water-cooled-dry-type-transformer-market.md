<p><p>Water-cooled dry type transformers are categorized primarily into two market types: casting type and lapping type. </p><p>Casting type transformers utilize a resin-encapsulated process, providing enhanced insulation and cooling, which is ideal for high-voltage applications. They offer robustness and are suitable for harsh environments. </p><p>On the other hand, lapping type transformers feature a layered design, promoting efficient heat dissipation through water cooling. This design is typically less bulky, making it suitable for urban installations where space is limited. Both types ensure reliability and efficiency in electrical systems.</p></p>
