<p><p>The upright vacuum market is predominantly categorized into two types: bagged and bagless models. Bagged vacuums utilize disposable bags to collect dirt and debris, promoting cleaner filtration and preventing dust re-emission during emptying. Conversely, bagless vacuums employ reusable containers, which can be emptied and washed, reducing ongoing costs associated with purchasing bags. Each type offers distinct advantages, with bagged vacuums generally favored for allergen control, while bagless options appeal to consumers for convenience and eco-friendliness.</p></p>
