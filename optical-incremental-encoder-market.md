<p><p>The optical incremental encoder market encompasses two primary types: incremental rotary encoders and incremental linear encoders. Incremental rotary encoders convert rotational position into electrical signals, commonly used in motors and robotics for precise movement control. In contrast, incremental linear encoders measure linear displacement, providing accurate position tracking in applications like CNC machinery and conveyor systems. Both types rely on optical sensing technology to deliver high-resolution feedback, enhancing performance in automation and motion control systems across various industries.</p></p>
