<p><p>YAG solid laser cutting machines operate primarily in two distinct market types: continuous lasers and pulse lasers. Continuous lasers emit a steady beam of light, ideal for high-speed cutting and processing of materials like metals, delivering consistent results. Conversely, pulse lasers generate short, intense bursts of energy, which excel in intricate cutting applications, providing precision without excessive heat, thus minimizing thermal distortion. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing productivity and precision in material fabrication processes.</p></p>
