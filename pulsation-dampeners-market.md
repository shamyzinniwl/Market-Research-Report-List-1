<p><p>The pulsation dampeners market features two main types: diaphragm and bladder. Diaphragm type dampeners utilize a flexible membrane to separate the processed fluid from a gas chamber, effectively absorbing pressure fluctuations. Bladder type dampeners, on the other hand, employ a flexible bladder filled with gas to achieve similar effects. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and food processing, as they enhance equipment longevity and improve operational efficiency by minimizing pressure surges.</p></p>
