<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Membrane Filtration Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration (MF)</li><li>Ultrafiltration (UF)</li><li>Nanofiltration (NF)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane filtration systems encompass various technologies that separate particles from liquids based on size and molecular weight. Microfiltration (MF) removes larger particles and microorganisms, typically above 0.1 micrometers. Ultrafiltration (UF) targets smaller solutes like proteins and colloids, with a cut-off of 1-100 nanometers. Nanofiltration (NF) handles salts and small organic molecules, while Reverse Osmosis (RO) ensures the removal of nearly all dissolved solids and contaminants by allowing only water molecules to pass through. Each type serves unique applications across industries.</p></p>
