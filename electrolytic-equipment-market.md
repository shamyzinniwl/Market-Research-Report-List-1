<p><p>The electrolytic equipment market is categorized into three types: acidity electrolytic, alkaline electrolytic, and neuter electrolytic systems. Acidity electrolytic equipment operates in acidic environments, facilitating processes like metal recovery and wastewater treatment. Alkaline electrolytic systems function in basic conditions, commonly used for chlorine and hydrogen production. Neuter electrolytic equipment operates in neutral pH environments, ideal for applications such as electroplating and surface treatment. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, contributing to advancements in various sectors.</p></p>
