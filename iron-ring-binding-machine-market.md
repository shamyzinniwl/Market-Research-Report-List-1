<p><strong>The Iron Ring Binding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2:1（21 Holes）</li><li>3:1（34 Holes）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Iron Ring Binding Machine market is categorized mainly into two types based on hole spacing: 2:1 and 3:1. The 2:1 (21 holes) binding offers a wider hole spacing, making it suitable for thicker documents, allowing for easier page turning and added durability. In contrast, the 3:1 (34 holes) binding features closer hole spacing, enabling a tighter, more compact binding suitable for slimmer documents, providing a neat appearance and greater alignment for professional presentations. These distinctions cater to diverse binding needs in various industries.</p></p>
