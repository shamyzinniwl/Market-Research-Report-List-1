<p><p>The hyperbaric oxygen chamber market comprises two main types: monoplace and multiplace chambers. Monoplace chambers are designed to accommodate a single patient, providing a controlled environment for oxygen therapy. They are compact and often used in outpatient settings. In contrast, multiplace chambers can hold multiple patients simultaneously, allowing for group treatments and enhanced monitoring by medical staff. This type is typically found in hospitals or specialized clinics, catering to more complex treatments and emergency situations.</p></p>
