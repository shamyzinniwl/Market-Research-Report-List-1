<p><p>The psoriatic arthritis therapeutics market comprises various treatment types. Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) alleviate pain and inflammation. Disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs) target the underlying disease process to prevent joint damage. Biologic drugs, derived from living organisms, specifically block pathways in the immune system to reduce symptoms and progression. Enzyme inhibitors focus on blocking enzymes that promote inflammation or joint damage, providing targeted treatment options to improve patient outcomes and manage symptoms effectively.</p></p>
