<p><strong>The Print Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Print Server</li><li>External Print Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Print servers are classified into two main types: internal and external. Internal print servers are built into printers, enhancing their functionality by allowing direct network connectivity and management without additional hardware. This integration simplifies deployment and reduces clutter. On the other hand, external print servers are separate devices that connect printers to a network, enabling multiple users to access a single printer. They provide flexibility and scalability, making them suitable for larger office environments or varied printer setups.</p></p>
