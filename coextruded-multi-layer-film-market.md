<p><p>Coextruded multi-layer films are categorized into two main types based on their structural characteristics: asymmetrical structure films and symmetrical structure films. Asymmetrical structure films have varying layer thicknesses and properties, allowing tailored performance for specific applications such as improved barrier properties or durability. In contrast, symmetrical structure films feature uniform layers, providing consistent performance and material distribution, making them ideal for packaging solutions requiring balanced strength and flexibility. Both types enhance functionality in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
