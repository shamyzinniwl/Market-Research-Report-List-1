<p><p>The sintered fly ash lightweight aggregates market is primarily segmented into Class F and Class C categories. Class F sintered fly ash is produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal, offering excellent durability and resistance to chemical attacks, making it ideal for high-performance concrete. Class C sintered fly ash comes from lignite coal and typically contains more calcium, providing benefits like improved strength and reduced water demand. Both types enhance the sustainability of construction materials and contribute to effective waste management.</p></p>
