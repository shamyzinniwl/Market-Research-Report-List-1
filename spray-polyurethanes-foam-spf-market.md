<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in construction, offering exceptional thermal insulation and air sealing properties. The market is categorized into open cell and closed cell types. Open cell SPF has a sponge-like structure that allows air and moisture to pass through, making it ideal for interior applications and soundproofing. Closed cell SPF is more rigid and impermeable, providing higher R-values and moisture resistance, suitable for exterior applications. Other types may include specialty formulations for specific uses.</p></p>
