<p><p>The oat flakes market comprises three main types: Instant Oats, Quick Oats, and Steel-cut Oats. Instant Oats are pre-cooked and dehydrated, allowing for a quick preparation time, often requiring just hot water. Quick Oats are steamed and rolled thinner, providing faster cooking compared to regular oats. Steel-cut Oats are whole oat groats chopped into pieces, offering a chewy texture and longer cooking time. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences for convenience and cooking methods.</p></p>
