<p><p>The Medium and High Voltage Testing Market encompasses various testing types essential for ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical systems. Sustained Low Frequency Tests examine equipment performance at reduced frequencies, while Constant DC Tests assess insulation properties under steady direct current. High Frequency Tests evaluate dielectric breakdown at elevated frequencies, and Surge or Impulse Tests simulate transient conditions to inspect the resilience of electrical components against voltage spikes. Each method is crucial for maintenance and compliance in electrical applications.</p></p>
