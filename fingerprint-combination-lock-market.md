<p><p>The Fingerprint Combination Lock market is primarily categorized into two types: Optical Scanner and Ultrasound. Optical scanners utilize light to capture fingerprint patterns, providing high accuracy and speed in authentication. They are widely used in consumer and security applications. In contrast, ultrasound fingerprint locks employ sound waves to create detailed 3D images of fingerprints, enhancing resistance to spoofing and improving overall security. Both technologies cater to different user needs, with optical being more common and ultrasound offering advanced protection features.</p></p>
