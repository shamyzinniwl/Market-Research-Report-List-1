<p><p>The stem cell antibody market is categorized into primary and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are designed to directly bind to specific stem cell antigens, facilitating the detection and analysis of stem cell properties in research and therapeutic applications. Secondary antibodies, on the other hand, bind to primary antibodies and are often conjugated with labels or enzymes for enhanced visualization. Together, these antibodies are essential for studying stem cell behavior, differentiation, and potential applications in regenerative medicine.</p></p>
