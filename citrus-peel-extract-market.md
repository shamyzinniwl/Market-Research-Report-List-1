<p><p>The Citrus Peel Extract market is segmented into two main types: oil and powder. Citrus peel oil, extracted through cold-pressing or steam distillation, is rich in essential oils and widely used in cosmetics, food flavoring, and aromatherapy. Conversely, citrus peel powder is created by drying and grinding the peels, retaining nutrients and antioxidants, making it popular in dietary supplements and health foods. Both forms offer unique benefits, catering to various industries such as food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
