<p><ul><li>Acidic Material</li><li>Alkaline Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer thinning fluid market is categorized into two types: acidic and alkaline materials. Acidic materials typically include solutions like sulfuric acid and hydrofluoric acid, which effectively etch and thin silicon wafers while reducing surface roughness. Alkaline materials, such as potassium hydroxide and ammonium hydroxide, are used for their ability to provide precise control over the thinning process and ensure uniformity. Both types are crucial for semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing the efficiency and performance of integrated circuits.</p></p>
