<p><p>The Commercial Automotive HMI market is primarily categorized into two types: Standard HMI and Multimodal HMI. Standard HMI focuses on traditional interfaces such as touchscreens and buttons, enabling basic driver interaction with the vehicle's systems. In contrast, Multimodal HMI incorporates various interaction methods, including voice recognition, gesture control, and visual displays, enhancing user experience by allowing drivers to interact with the vehicle seamlessly and intuitively.
