<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multiple Points</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer manifolds are essential in controlling the flow of draft beer from kegs to taps. There are two primary types: single point and multiple point. A single point manifold serves one tap, managing beer from a single keg, ideal for simplicity and limited selection. In contrast, a multiple point manifold connects multiple taps to various kegs, allowing for a diverse beer selection. This setup is beneficial for bars and events, enabling efficient distribution and a broader variety of beer offerings.</p></p>
