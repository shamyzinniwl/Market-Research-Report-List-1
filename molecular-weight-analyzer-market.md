<p><strong>The Molecular Weight Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC)</li><li>Gas Chromatography & Mass Spec (GC/MS)</li><li>Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC)</li><li>High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC)</li><li>Differential Refractive Index (DRI)</li><li>Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular weight analyzer market encompasses various technologies for characterizing polymers and large biomolecules. Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC) separates molecules based on size in a gel medium, while Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC) utilizes a similar principle without high pressures. Gas Chromatography & Mass Spectrometry (GC/MS) combines the separation of volatile compounds with mass identification. High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) offers precise quantification of components, while Differential Refractive Index (DRI) detects concentration changes. Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS) provides absolute molecular weight data through light scattering techniques.</p></p>
