<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Power Generator</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Power Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power generator market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal-axis and vertical-axis wind power generators. Horizontal-axis wind turbines, which have blades that rotate around a horizontal shaft, are the most common and efficient, suited for large-scale wind farms. In contrast, vertical-axis wind turbines feature blades that move around a vertical shaft, making them versatile for urban environments and low-wind areas. Both types play crucial roles in harnessing renewable energy, catering to diverse applications and geographic conditions.</p></p>
