<p><strong>The Medical CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical CMOS image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Front-Side Illumination (FSI) and Back-Side Illumination (BSI). FSI sensors capture images through the front surface, benefiting from simpler design and cost-effectiveness but often with inferior light sensitivity. In contrast, BSI sensors allow light to reach the photodiodes from the back, enhancing sensitivity and low-light performance, making them ideal for medical imaging applications requiring high resolution and clarity, like endoscopy and surgical imaging.</p></p>
