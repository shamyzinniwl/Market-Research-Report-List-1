<p><p>The crop sprayers market can be categorized into three main types based on application volume. Ultra Low Volume (ULV) sprayers, utilizing less than 5 liters per hectare, are designed for precise applications with minimal chemical use. Low Volume sprayers operate between 5 to 400 liters per hectare, providing efficient coverage while reducing waste. High Volume sprayers, using more than 400 liters per hectare, are suited for extensive agricultural practices requiring thorough treatment over larger areas. Each type serves specific agricultural needs and strategies.</p></p>
