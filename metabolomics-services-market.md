<p><p>The metabolomics services market is primarily divided into targeted and untargeted metabolomics services. Targeted metabolomics focuses on quantifying specific metabolites of interest, providing detailed insights into particular biochemical pathways or physiological responses. This approach is often used in disease diagnostics and biomarker discovery. In contrast, untargeted metabolomics aims to profile a broad spectrum of metabolites without prior knowledge, allowing for a comprehensive overview of metabolic changes. This method is valuable for exploratory research and discovering new biomarkers.</p></p>
