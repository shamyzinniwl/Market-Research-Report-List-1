<p><ul><li>Dry Type Three-phase Isolation Transformer</li><li>Oil-immersed Three-phase Isolation Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase isolation transformer market consists of two primary types: dry type and oil-immersed. Dry type three-phase isolation transformers utilize air for cooling and are suitable for indoor applications due to their lower risk of environmental hazards. They are often employed in commercial and industrial settings. In contrast, oil-immersed three-phase isolation transformers use oil for cooling, offering higher efficiency and capacity, making them ideal for outdoor applications and heavy-duty industrial use. Both types enhance electrical safety and system reliability.</p></p>
