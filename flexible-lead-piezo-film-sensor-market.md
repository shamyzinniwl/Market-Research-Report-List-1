<p><p>The Flexible Lead Piezo Film Sensor market is divided into two key types: surface and implant. Surface sensors are designed for applications requiring external monitoring, such as wearable devices and industrial equipment, capturing vibrations and pressure changes on the surface they are applied to. In contrast, implant sensors are intended for medical applications, embedded within the body to monitor physiological conditions closely. Both types leverage the piezoelectric properties of the film to convert mechanical stress into electrical signals, enhancing monitoring capabilities in their respective fields.</p></p>
