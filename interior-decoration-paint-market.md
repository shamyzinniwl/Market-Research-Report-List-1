<p><strong>The Interior Decoration Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior decoration paint market is primarily divided into two types: waterborne and solvent-borne paints. Waterborne paints use water as a primary solvent, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), faster drying times, and easy cleanup with soap and water. They are environmentally friendly and ideal for indoor use. Conversely, solvent-borne paints contain organic solvents, providing durability and superior adhesion. However, they have higher VOC levels, longer drying times, and require solvents for cleaning. Both types cater to diverse aesthetic and performance needs.</p></p>
