<p><ul><li>PNP Output</li><li>NPN Output</li><li>OSE Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photoelectric Safety Protection Device market includes three main types based on output: PNP, NPN, and OSE. PNP output devices provide a high signal when activated, commonly used in applications requiring safety isolation. NPN output devices, conversely, provide a low signal, ideal for interfacing with grounded systems. OSE (Optical Safety Enhancement) output devices focus on enhanced signal integrity and safety in challenging conditions. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, ensuring reliable safety measures in automation processes.</p></p>
