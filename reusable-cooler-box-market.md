<p><p>The reusable cooler box market is segmented into various types based on materials, each offering unique benefits. PU Foam provides excellent insulation and durability, making it ideal for extended temperature retention. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for disposable or single-use applications. Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) offers higher insulation performance and water resistance, while Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) is known for its flexibility and impact resistance. Other materials include specialized composites and biodegradable options, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
