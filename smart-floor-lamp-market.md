<p><strong>The Smart Floor Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-light Floor Lamp</li><li>Upward-light Floor Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart floor lamp market features two primary types: direct-light floor lamps and upward-light floor lamps. Direct-light floor lamps focus illumination downward, making them ideal for tasks like reading or working, while providing targeted brightness. In contrast, upward-light floor lamps direct light towards the ceiling, creating a softer ambient glow that enhances the overall atmosphere of a room. Both types integrate smart technology, allowing users to control brightness, color, and scheduling through apps or voice commands, enhancing convenience and lifestyle.</p></p>
