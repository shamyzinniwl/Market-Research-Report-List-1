<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Anti Corrosion Oils</li><li>Water-based Anti Corrosion Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-corrosion oils market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based anti-corrosion oils. Solvent-based anti-corrosion oils use organic solvents as carriers, providing robust protection against rust and corrosion while offering fast drying times. They are typically used in industrial applications requiring strong adhesion and surface coverage. In contrast, water-based anti-corrosion oils are formulated with water as the primary solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean, yet still effective in providing corrosion resistance for various applications.</p></p>
