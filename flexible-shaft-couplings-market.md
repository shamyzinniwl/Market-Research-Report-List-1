<p><p>The flexible shaft couplings market includes several types designed to accommodate misalignment and shock loads. Elastomeric couplings utilize rubber-like materials to absorb vibration and provide flexibility. Gear couplings feature toothed gears that allow for angular misalignment while transmitting torque. Bellow couplings use thin-walled bellows to achieve torsional flexibility and compensate for misalignments. Disc couplings employ flexible metal discs to transmit torque and manage misalignment with high precision. Other types encompass various specialized designs catering to specific applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
