<p><p>Steam injection humidifiers are categorized into automatic and manual types based on their operational mechanisms. Automatic steam injection humidifiers utilize sensors to monitor humidity levels and adjust water and steam output accordingly, ensuring optimal indoor air quality with minimal user intervention. In contrast, manual steam injection humidifiers require users to set humidity levels and control steam output directly, offering more hands-on management. Both types serve essential roles in industries and applications where precise humidity control is crucial.</p></p>
