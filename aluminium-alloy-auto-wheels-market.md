<p><p>The aluminium alloy auto wheels market can be categorized into three main types: casting wheels, forging wheels, and others. </p><p>Casting wheels are created by pouring molten aluminium into molds, allowing for intricate designs and cost-effectiveness. Forging wheels are made by shaping solid aluminium under high pressure, resulting in enhanced strength and reduced weight, ideal for performance vehicles. Other wheels include specialized manufacturing processes like rolling or machining, catering to specific aesthetic or performance requirements, thus broadening the options for consumers and manufacturers alike.</p></p>
