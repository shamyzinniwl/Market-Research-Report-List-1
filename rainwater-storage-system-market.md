<p><p>The Rainwater Storage System market includes two primary types: Modular Storage Tank Systems and Channel Systems. Modular Storage Tank Systems consist of interlocking tanks that can be customized in size and configuration, allowing flexibility for different space and water volume requirements. Channel Systems, on the other hand, involve collecting rainwater in linear channels or trenches, directing it to storage areas, and efficiently maximizing the use of available land. Both systems aim to enhance water conservation and sustainable management.</p></p>
